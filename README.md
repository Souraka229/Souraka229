## `> cat manifesto.txt`

Most global platforms treat African markets as an afterthought.

No offline-first design.  
No local payment rails.  
No real understanding of how business moves on the ground.

I'm not building *for* Africa from the outside.  
I'm building **from inside** — which means the constraints are the product.

<div align="center">

*"The gap between global tech and African needs*  
*isn't a problem. It's the opportunity."*

<br/>

**If you're a young African who thinks the best opportunities**  
**are reserved for the right backgrounds — apply anyway.**

🌍 **The world is watching Africa.**

</div>
